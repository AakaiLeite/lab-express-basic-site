The Grupo Lobo

The Grupo Lobo is a non-governmental environmental organization (NGO), independent and non-profit, founded on September 18, 1985, and recognized with the status of Public Utility. As part of the Signatus Program, the Wolf Group has initiated a strategy with areas of action that focus on informing the public opinion, supporting scientific studies, and promoting practical conservation measures.
