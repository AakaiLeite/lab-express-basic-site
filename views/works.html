  • Fostering Public Support: One of Grupo Lobo's primary missions is to inform the public about the importance of wolf conservation and the ecological role that wolves play in maintaining healthy ecosystems. They engage in educational programs, public events, and awareness campaigns to dispel myths and misconceptions about wolves while emphasizing their significance in the balance of nature.
  • Support for Scientific Studies: To further the understanding of wolf behavior, ecology, and population dynamics, Grupo Lobo actively supports scientific research on the Iberian wolf. By collaborating with researchers and institutions, they contribute to the growing body of knowledge about these fascinating animals.
  • Practical Conservation Measures: Grupo Lobo is committed to implementing practical conservation measures to protect the Iberian wolf population. Through habitat restoration initiatives, promotion of coexistence between humans and wolves, and efforts to reduce illegal hunting and poaching, the organization seeks to secure a sustainable future for the species.
  • The Iberian Wolf Recovery Center: The Iberian Wolf Recovery Center, also known as Centro de Recuperação do Lobo Ibérico (CRLI), is another vital organization focused on wolf conservation in Portugal. The center is dedicated to the rehabilitation of injured or orphaned wolves with the aim of releasing them back into the wild whenever possible.
  • Rehabilitation and Release Program: At the Iberian Wolf Recovery Center, expert veterinarians and animal care specialists work tirelessly to nurse injured wolves back to health. Similarly, orphaned wolf cubs are provided with a nurturing environment until they are old enough to be released into the wild, where they can once again thrive in their natural habitat.
